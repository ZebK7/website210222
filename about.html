People

We are an inclusive working environment, investing in our local communities, and supporting the people at the heart of our business.

Coffee

To continue making the coffee that you love, we're investing in a sustainable coffee supply from the Amazon and jungles of Papua New Guinea, and supporting Pygmie communities behind our coffee.

Planet

To keep inspiring the world to love great coffee, we must respect the planet and ensure we reduce our environmental footprint, at every step.

We use recycled cups and cutlery and all our furniture is environmentally sourced bamboo!
